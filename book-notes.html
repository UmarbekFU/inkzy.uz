Book December 2024 9/10

Atomic Habits

by James Clear

The 1% improvement rule is everything. Small changes compound over time. The key insight: focus on systems, not goals.

Habits are the compound interest of self-improvement. The difference between success and failure is often not dramatic changes, but small, consistent improvements.

Key takeaways: Identity-based habits are more powerful than outcome-based ones. The environment shapes behavior more than motivation. Stacking habits creates powerful routines.
